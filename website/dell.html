Dell: The Innovation Imperative

To the disrupters go the spoils. Those who disrupt industries can change consumer behavior, alter economics, and transform lives. How did the once-innovative Dell lose its edge, and how can it get it back? This study answers that question, and proposes that Dell stay private and adopt an Intelligent Gambler’s© approach to innovation – namely investing in other companies to effectively leverage its significant capital and take intelligent risks in a fast-changing world.
